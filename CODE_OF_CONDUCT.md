# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is a learning space for AI education, medical research workflows, and open-source course design. Please keep discussion respectful, specific, and useful.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- explain disagreements with evidence
- respect beginners and non-programmers
- keep medical safety boundaries clear
- help contributors make their work easier to review
- credit ideas and sources when appropriate

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- sharing private medical, identity, credential, or patient data
- encouraging unsafe clinical use of the project
- spam, unrelated promotion, or deliberately disruptive comments

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.

## Reporting

If you see a problem, open an issue or contact the maintainer through the GitHub profile associated with this repository. Do not include private medical or identity information in public issues.
